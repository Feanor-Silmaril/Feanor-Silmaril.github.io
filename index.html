Wartime Accounts

6 of the bloodiest battles were fought over the Silmarils.

The Battles of Beleriand for the Silmarils lasted from YT 1497 up to the very end of the First Age.


First Battle (Year of the Trees 1497)

Soon after the end of his imprisonment, Morgoth laid siege upon the realm of Thingol. This is the only battle in which the Laiquendi fought with the Sindar, choosing to remain isolated after the victory of the battle and the loss of their beloved king Denethor. The battle was fought at Eglarest in the West and in the East above Andram between Aros and Gelion.

Second Battle “Dagor-nuin-Giliath” (Year of the Trees 1497)

After the Noldor, led by Feanor, arrived at the coast, they foolishly chose to burn the ships of the Teleri behind them. Alerted by the smoke of the Teleri ships Morgoth commands his army to advance to the Noldor's location. The battle that ensued was swift. Despite the overwhelming odds the Noldor defeated the forces of Morgoth and claimed Mithrim from the orcs. In his lust for vengeance the king of the Noldor pursued Morgoth, but he was no match for Morgoth’s forces and was severely wounded by Gothmog, Lord of the Balrogs. Feanor would soon perish, and his sons followed in his ruinous path vowing to avenge their father’s death and recover the Silmarils.

Third Battle “Dagor Aglareb” (F.A. Year 60)

Without precedent Morgoth deployed an invasion across Beleriand. He sent his Orcs to the Pass of Sirion in the west and Maglor’s Gap in the east pushing far South into Beleriand. There was great destruction by the hand of the Orcs but ultimately Maedros and Fingolfin mounted a successful counterattack and drove out the attackers into Ard-galen leaving few surviving orcs. The battle resulted in a decimation of Morgoth's forces and a period of peace for Beleriand.

Fourth Battle "Dagor Bragollach", (F.A. Year 455-456)

For centuries Morgoth's forces were kept at bay in his stronghold, Angband. However, his restraint could not hold Morgoth eternally and after centuries of restrainment Morgoth once again deployed his forces throughout Beleriand. Morgoth devastated Ardgalen, conquered Maglor's Gap, and ultimately gained domain of many of the Northern lands. This battle marked the first expansion of Morgoth's power and the beginning of a dark time for Beleriand.

Fifth Battle (F.A. Year 472)

Morgoth’s forces (the Orcs) broke into two hosts, one passing west through the vale of Sirion and the other passing east between Aros and Gelion, some of them even scaling the passes of Anarch and Aglon.

Sixth Battle (F.A. Year 545-587)

Morgoth's power reigned in Beleriand. Despair permeated the land of Beleriand. Using the light of a Simaril to guide him to Western lands, Earendil travels to Valinor to plea for the aid of the Valar in opposing Morgoth. The Valar agreed to help and retaliated against the forces of Morgoth. One of the most epic battles that has been seen in Arda broke out, at some points it seemed that all was lost but ultimately the Valar and the coalition that supported them emerged as the victors. The stress of the battle was too great for the lands of Beleriand, the world would be irrevocably changed.
