Wartime Accounts

6 of the bloodiest battles were fought over the Silmarils.

The Battles of Beleriand for the Silmarils lasted from YT 1497 up to the very end of the First Age.


First Battle (Year of the Trees 1497)

Soon after the end of his imprisonment, Morgoth laid siege upon the realm of Thingol. This is the only battle in which the Laiquendi fought with the Sindar, choosing to remain isolated after the victory of the battle and the loss of their beloved king Denethor. The battle was fought at Eglarest in the West and in the East above Andram between Aros and Gelion.

Second Battle (Year of the Trees 1497)

After the Noldor, led by Feanor, arrived at the coast, they foolishly chose to burn the ships of the Teleri behind them. Alerted by the smoke of the Teleri ships Morgoth commands his army to advance to the Noldor's location. The battle that ensued was swift. Despite the overwhelming odds the Noldor defeated the forces of Morgoth and claimed Mithrim from the orcs. In his lust for vengeance the king of the Noldor pursued Morgoth, but he was no match for Morgoth’s forces and was severely wounded by Gothmog, Lord of the Balrogs. Feanor would soon perish, and his sons followed in his ruinous path vowing to avenge their father’s death and recover the Silmarils.

Third Battle (F.A. Year 60)

The battle began when the Ñoldor of Fëanor had arrived unexpectedly at the Firth of Drengist, and passed through the Gate of the Noldor into Hithlum. They encamped on the northern shore of Lake Mithrim.

Fourth Battle (F.A. Year 455-456)

The battle began when the Ñoldor of Fëanor had arrived unexpectedly at the Firth of Drengist, and passed through the Gate of the Noldor into Hithlum. They encamped on the northern shore of Lake Mithrim.

Fifth Battle (F.A. Year 472)

Morgoth’s forces (the Orcs) broke into two hosts, one passing west through the vale of Sirion and the other passing east between Aros and Gelion, some of them even scaling the passes of Anarch and Aglon.

Sixth Battle (F.A. Year 545-587)

Morgoth’s forces (the Orcs) broke into two hosts, one passing west through the vale of Sirion and the other passing east between Aros and Gelion, some of them even scaling the passes of Anarch and Aglon.
